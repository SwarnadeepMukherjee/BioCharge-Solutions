**Note:-


If using WINDOWS, please install BlueStacks 5 from

here

before performing the following steps



Step 1: Download the app from here and go to 'Downloads'



Step 2: Open the file named 'ArduTooth-BioCharge-Solutions.apk'



Step 3: Select 'Install with package Installer' as 'Just once'



Step 4: Click on settings



Step 5: Turn on 'Allow from this source'



Step 6: Open the file again and click on 'Install' and then 'Done'



Step 7: Click on 'Pair new device'



Step 8: Click 'HC-05'



Step 9: Enter passcode '1234' and click 'OK'



Step 10: Open the app and click 'HC-05'



Step 11: Click on 'Receiver'



Step 12: Click on the red-circled button to link with the device



Step 13: The app is ready to show the data. Sensor 1 shows Voltage and Sensor 2 shows Efficiency



Step 14: If needed, the names of sensors can be changed by going to the settings



Step 15: And, renaming the Sensors manually. (Sensor 1 as Voltage and Sensor 2 as Efficiency)



Step 16: The sensors are renamed successfully
